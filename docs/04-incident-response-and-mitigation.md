## Incident Response Actions

The response focused on containing the incident, restoring affected services, and preventing further damage to the infrastructure.

## Response Challenges

- No predefined incident response procedures
- Limited visibility into affected systems
- Dependence on manual analysis

## Mitigation Measures

- Isolation of affected systems
- Restoration of services
- Identification of security control gaps

## Outcome

While services were eventually restored, the incident highlighted the need for structured response planning and improved security monitoring.
