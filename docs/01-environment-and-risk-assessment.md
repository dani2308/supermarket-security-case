## Environment and Business Context

The analyzed environment represents a supermarket enterprise relying on interconnected information systems to support daily operations such as inventory management, sales processing, and supplier communication.

Due to the business-critical nature of these systems, availability, integrity, and confidentiality are essential to ensure operational continuity.

## Identified Risk Factors

- High dependency on IT systems for daily operations
- Limited security governance and formal procedures
- Absence of continuous monitoring mechanisms

## Security Impact

These factors create a fragile security posture where failures or attacks can directly affect business operations and customer trust.
